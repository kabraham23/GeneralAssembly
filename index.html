Hi! I'm Katie! I studied music my whole life, and I pursued opera as a professional career for 10 years. I love singing and music and the bonds you make along the way.
After a few worn-out suitcases, and many nights sleeping in hotels, I thought it was time to hang up my Valkyrie horns and settle down. I got two cats, Trixie and Katya, and I nested.
Now I have a job as an assistant. That's what everyone does with a Masters degree in Opera. I am looking forward to pursuing a career in Full-Stack Development!
As you can see, I have a very vintage 70s aesthetic, and I like cooking and crafts. I recently started making plant hangers for my friend's who don't have animals on a mission to kill all plants. I live by the words of the Great Jonathan Van Ness, "Im just trying to make a better life for my cats."
